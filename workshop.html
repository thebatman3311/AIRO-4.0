Gen AI Forge

Where Quick Thinking meets Swift Coding!


This workshop is designed to inspire creativity and innovation among participants by introducing them to the exciting world of Generative AI. Through hands-on activities and collaborative tasks, participants will explore the capabilities of AI in generating creative content. The workshop emphasizes learning, experimentation, and collaboration, allowing participants to discover new possibilities with AI tools.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Individual Participation: Only individual participation is allowed.
  2. Equipment Provided: Computers and internet connection will be provided.
  3. Co-operation: Participants are required to give their full co-operation to the organizers for gaining good insights.
  4. Rule Modifications: The workshop organizers reserve the right to modify the rules and format if necessary.
