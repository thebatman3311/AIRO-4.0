Dead Fold

Where Quick Thinking meets Swift Coding!


This event is designed to bring joy and excitement to all participants. It features a variety of engaging activities and games that encourage laughter, camaraderie, and a spirited atmosphere. The event emphasizes teamwork, communication, and trust as the participants work together to achieve their goal.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Team Composition: Each team must contain only 2 members.
  2. No External Help: No external help or communication devices are allowed during the event.
  3. Disqualification: Any team found receiving outside assistance will be disqualified.
  4. Rule Modifications: The event organisers reserve the right to modify the rules and format if necessary.
  5. Behaviour: Any form of inappropriate behaviour, including arguing, cheating, or disrupting the event, will result in immediate disqualification.
  6. Scoring: Points will be awarded based on performance, teamwork, and adherence to the event's spirit. Teams with the highest scores will be declared the winners.
