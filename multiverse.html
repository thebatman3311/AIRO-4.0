Multiverse Of Madness

Where Quick Thinking meets Swift Coding!


Welcome to the Multiverse of Madness, a thrilling and fun event designed to challenge your wit and teamwork! In this exciting event, teams of 2 or 3 will embark on a journey through two rounds filled with mind-bending statements and hilarious actions. Get ready to test your ability to spot the truth and communicate through actions as you battle it out for victory!

Event Details

Event Type: Team event consisting of 2 or 3 participants

Round 1: Bluff or Belief

  1. Each team will be presented with two statements: one true and one false.
  2. The team must identify the correct statement.
  3. If they choose correctly, they move forward; if not, they face a funny punishment.
  4. The top teams from this round will qualify for the next round.

Round 2: Mimic Madness
