SpiderTech Web

Where Quick Thinking meets Swift Coding!


Join us at SpiderTech Web for a cutting-edge event on modern web technologies! Dive into the latest innovations, frameworks, and tools shaping the industry. Engage in dynamic presentations, interactive sessions, and gain practical knowledge to enhance user experiences and streamline development processes. Whether refining your skills or exploring new tech avenues, SpiderTech Web is your platform for growth and discovery.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Each team must consist of exactly 3 members.
  2. Teams will be provided with a basic, static webpage as the starting point.
  3. Transform the provided static webpage into an advanced and dynamic webpage using the platform provided (Visual Studio Code).
  4. Participants do not need to bring their own laptops. Computers will be provided for use during the event.
  5. The event will be held within a specified time limit. The exact duration will be communicated prior to the event.
  6. During the event, teams will be given specific tasks or challenges that must be completed. These tasks are designed to test various aspects of web development.
  7. Tasks must be completed in sequence and integrated into the final webpage. Failure to complete a task may affect the overall score.
  8. Teams are allowed to use any technology or framework compatible with the provided platform. However, any external libraries or plugins must be pre-approved by the event organizers.
  9. Teams can use AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, etc., to build the webpages.
  10. The final webpage should be functional, user-friendly, and visually appealing. Adherence to modern web design standards is encouraged.
  11. Teams may be disqualified for violating any of the event rules, including unsportsmanlike conduct or unauthorized use of external resources like GitHub.
